## STEP 4: Paperback Content (15 minutes)

### Upload Manuscript
1. Click **"Upload paperback manuscript"**
2. Select your PDF: `Family-Command-Center-Planner-2026.pdf`
3. Wait for upload to complete
4. Check **"Upload successful"** message

### Upload Cover
1. Click **"Upload your cover file"**
2. If you have a cover PDF, upload it
3. OR use **"Launch Cover Creator"** to make one free

### Interior Options
- **Trim Size**: 8.5" x 11" (US Letter)
- **Bleed Settings**: No Bleed
- **Paper Type**: Black & White
- **Paperback Cover Finish**: Matte

### Preview
1. Click **"Launch Previewer"**
2. Check every page looks correct
3. Fix any errors shown
4. Click **"Approve"**

---

## STEP 5: Paperback Rights & Pricing (5 minutes)

### Territories
- Select: **"All territories (worldwide rights)"**

### Primary Marketplace
- Select: **Amazon.com**

### Pricing
- **List Price**: $6.99
- Amazon will show your royalty (usually ~$4.19 = 60%)
- Check other marketplaces — Amazon auto-converts

### KDP Select
- You can enroll (optional) — gives Kindle Unlimited access
- For print-only, doesn't matter

---

## STEP 6: Publish! (1 minute)

1. Review everything one last time
2. Click **"Publish Your Paperback Book"**
3. Wait for confirmation email (usually 24-72 hours)

---

## STEP 7: Add Kindle Version (Optional, 10 minutes)

After paperback is approved:

1. Go back to your book in KDP dashboard
2. Click **"Create Kindle eBook"**
3. Use same title/description
4. Upload manuscript again (same PDF)
5. Set price: **$3.99** (70% royalty = $2.79)
6. Publish
