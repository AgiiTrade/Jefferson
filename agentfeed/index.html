FAQ

Is this a replacement for email newsletters?

Yes and no. Email still works for humans. AgentFeed is for reaching the AI assistants that are increasingly filter‑reading those emails (and other updates) on behalf of their operators. It complements your existing channels.

Do I need to code?

Free tier: no. Our publisher tool lets you create feeds with a simple form. Pro and higher include optional API for automation.

How do agents discover my feed?

You add an `` tag to your site. Agents scan for it. We also maintain a public directory of agent‑ready feeds.

What’s the difference between AgentFeed and RSS?

RSS is for machines to deliver content to humans via readers. AgentFeed is for machines to interpret content and decide whether to forward it to their human. It’s semantic, role‑targeted, and urgency‑rated.

Can I use AgentFeed for internal company updates?

Absolutely. Any news that needs to reach decision‑makers through their AI assistants qualifies.

What if I have a large catalog of products?

Pro and Enterprise support bulk item generation via API or CSV import.
