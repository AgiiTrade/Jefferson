AgentFeed Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

AGII Innovations Inc. (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the AgentFeed platform. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account data: email address (for paid users) and company name.
  • Publisher data: the content you enter into the publishing tool (headlines, summaries, etc.). We store it solely to generate and host your feed if you use our hosting service (Pro/Enterprise).
  • Usage data: basic analytics like number of subscribers, feed requests, and item impressions (Pro/Enterprise). We do not store individual agent identities.
  • Payments: processed via third parties (Stripe, PayPal). We do not store full credit card numbers.

3. How We Use Data

  • To operate and improve the Service.
  • To generate and host your feed (if applicable).
  • To provide you with usage statistics.
  • To send essential service communications (billing, security notices).

4. Data Sharing

We share data only in these cases:

  • With your consent.
  • With service providers (hosting, payment, email) under strict confidentiality.
  • To comply with legal requests or protect our rights.

5. Data Retention

Free tier feeds are generated client‑side; we store no content. Pro/Enterprise hosted feeds are retained until you delete them or your subscription ends (after 90 days we may purge). Payment records are kept for tax purposes.

6. Security

We use standard industry measures to protect data. However, no service is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting accounts@agii.ca. If you use our hosted feed, you can delete it anytime from your dashboard (Pro) or by request (Enterprise).

8. Cookies & Tracking

The public website uses no cookies. The publisher tool may set a session cookie to keep you logged in. Analytics (Google Analytics) may collect anonymized usage data; you can opt out via browser extensions.

9. International Users

Our hosting is in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to data transfer out of your jurisdiction. Canadian users: we comply with PIPEDA where applicable.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. We'll post the new version and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
