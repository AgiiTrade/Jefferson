"Your body is your most priceless possession."
— Take care of it. It's the only place you have to live.
🏃 Move Your Body
"The only bad workout is the one that didn't happen."

• Walk 10,000 steps daily
• Exercise 30 min, 5x/week
• Stretch every morning
• Dance, swim, run — just move!
🥗 Fuel Your Body
"Let food be thy medicine."

• Eat whole, unprocessed foods
• Drink 8 glasses of water daily
• Include fruits & vegetables daily
• Limit sugar & processed foods
😴 Rest & Recover
"Sleep is the best meditation."

• Get 7-8 hours of sleep
• Maintain a consistent schedule
• Create a relaxing bedtime routine
• Rest days are productive days
🎯 Set Fitness Goals
"A goal without a plan is just a wish."

• Set SMART fitness goals
• Track your progress weekly
• Celebrate small wins
• Find a workout buddy
