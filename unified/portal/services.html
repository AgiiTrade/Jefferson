❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. You'll only pay the difference between your current package and the new one.

How do consultation calls work?

Premium and Elite members can schedule 30-minute video calls with our education consultants. We cover application strategy, document review, and answer your questions.

Is the visa guidance comprehensive?

Elite package includes step-by-step guidance for Canadian student visa (study permit) applications, including document preparation, financial proof requirements, and application review.

Do you guarantee admission?

We cannot guarantee admission as decisions are made by universities. However, our guidance significantly improves your application quality and chances of acceptance.
