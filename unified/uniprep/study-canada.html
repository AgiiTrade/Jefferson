📋 Application Steps

1
Choose Your Program
Research programs that match your interests and career goals. Consider co-op options for work experience.
2
Meet Requirements
English proficiency (IELTS 6.5+), academic transcripts, letters of recommendation, statement of purpose.
3
Apply to Universities
Submit applications through university portals. Deadlines: Jan 15 (Fall), Sep 15 (Winter).
4
Get Your Study Permit
Apply through IRCC. Need: acceptance letter, financial proof, medical exam, biometrics.
5
Arrive & Start!
Attend orientation, get SIN, open bank account, find housing. Welcome to Canada! 🇨🇦
