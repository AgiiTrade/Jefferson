## 🎁 BONUS: Prompt Engineering Formula

Use this structure for ANY prompt:

```
[ROLE] You are a [expert type] with [X] years of experience in [field].

[CONTEXT] I am working on [project/situation] and need help with [specific task].

[TASK] Please [action verb] a [output type] that [requirements].

[CONSTRAINTS] The output should be:
- [Format requirement]
- [Length requirement]  
- [Tone/style requirement]
- [Include/exclude requirement]

[EXAMPLES] Here's an example of what I'm looking for:
[paste example]
```
