QuestMind Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-21

Overview

QuestMind is a quiz and learning game experience. We aim to keep data collection minimal.

Information we collect

QuestMind may store gameplay progress and settings locally on your device. If analytics or backend services are added later, this policy should be updated before wider release.

How information is used

Stored information is used to support gameplay continuity, progress, settings, and app improvement.

Children's privacy

Because QuestMind is intended for a broad audience including younger players, the app should avoid unnecessary personal data collection. Parents or guardians can contact support with questions.

Third-party services

If third-party analytics, ads, or account systems are introduced, those services and data practices must be disclosed here before release.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@agii.ca.
