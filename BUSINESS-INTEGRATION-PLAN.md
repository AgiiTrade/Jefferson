## 🏗️ UNIFIED SITE ARCHITECTURE

### Domain Strategy

| Option | URL | Pros | Cons |
|--------|-----|------|------|
| **A: Subdomain** | uniprep.studyncanada.com | Clean, one domain | Need DNS access |
| **B: Subfolder** | studyncanada.com/uniprep | Best for SEO | Slightly longer URL |

- Best SEO (all authority on one domain)
- Single brand identity
- Easier to manage
