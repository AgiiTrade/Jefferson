# 📖 Journal

## Description

A private digital journal for daily entries, gratitude practice, and personal reflection. Capture your thoughts, track your emotional journey, and build a meaningful record of your life.

## Features

- **Daily Entries** — Free-form writing with prompts
- **Gratitude Practice** — 3 things you're grateful for each day
- **Mood Tracking** — Emotional state logging with trends
- **Photo Journal** — Attach images to entries
- **Prompts Library** — Writing prompts for when you're stuck
- **Search & Tags** — Find entries by topic, mood, or date

## Setup Instructions

1. Create a new page: "Journal"
2. Create **Entries Database**:
   - Title (Title — auto-generates from date)
   - Date (Date)
   - Mood (Select: 😊 Happy, 😌 Calm, 🤔 Reflective, 😢 Sad, 😤 Frustrated, 🤩 Excited, 😴 Tired)
   - Energy (Select: ⚡ High, 🔋 Medium, 🪫 Low)
   - Gratitude 1 (Text)
   - Gratitude 2 (Text)
   - Gratitude 3 (Text)
   - Entry (Text — long, for free-form writing)
   - Highlight of the Day (Text)
   - Tomorrow's Intention (Text)
   - Photos (Files & Media)
   - Tags (Multi-select: Work, Family, Health, Travel, Creative, Learning)
3. Create Calendar View showing entries by date
4. Create Gallery View for photo journal

## Customization Tips

- Add **Morning Pages** and **Evening Reflection** as separate templates
- Use **Formulas** to track journaling streaks
- Create **Monthly Review** pages pulling from daily entries
- Add **Dream Journal** property for recording dreams
